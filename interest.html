Interests

Drum & Beatbox

I played the drum since nine years old. I started to learn Beatbox since 2013.

I am an introvert. Drum brings me power.

I was shy when I spoke in front of people. When I was nine, my mom asked me: "What instruments do you want to learn?" "Drum. It's so cool!" I replied. So I started to learn playing the drum kit during weekends. My mom is great. She not only wants me to learn something, but also wants me to be outgoing. I did several performances, but I was still nervous before I playing. When I played, I totally got into the flow state and didn't feel nervous any more.

Even now, I still feel nervous before doing a presentation. But I am confident that when I begin, I can overcome this.

Beatbox is very interesting; I can use my mouth to simulate the sound of the drum.
